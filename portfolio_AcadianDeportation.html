About

This project involved the creation of a vintage-style thematic map that tells the story of the 1755 Acadian Deportation.

The finished map depicts the East Coast of North America and is embedded with flow lines, spreadsheets, a deportation order, a cartouche, and other graphics.

Application

  • Esri ArcGIS Pro
  • Adobe Illustrator
  • Adobe Photoshop

Data

  • Acadian Deportation Data from
    Denis Hache.
  • Mitchell Map
  • Natural Resources Canada
  • U.S. Geological Survey

Details

Utilised the paintbrush tool in Illustrator to create hand drawn terrains.

Applied the various blending modes, patterns, and opacities to mimic vintage styles.

Created graphics in Photoshop consistent with the map style to provide additional information.

Implemented masks with blending modes in Photoshop to embed a cartouche from a historical map.
