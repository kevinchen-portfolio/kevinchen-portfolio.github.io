About

This project involved the production of a historical oblique-view map in downtown Fredericton, New Brunswick.

Using a COLLADA File, the existing 3D buildings were exported to SketchUp. After applying materials to the buildings, they were geolocated to ArcGIS Pro.

Finally, a 3D Scene was generated and rendered with vintage styles to create a map that evoked a historical mood.

Application

  • Trimble SketchUp Pro
  • Esri ArcGIS Pro

Data

  • OpenStreetMap
  • Style Files, Esri

Details

Randomized heights and styling of the background buildings.

Created 3D buildings on the map.

Added a vintage paper texture and a fold effect.

Embellished the map with trees.
