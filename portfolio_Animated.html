About

This project involved the production of an animated video to honour Terry Fox’s Marathon of Hope and to tell his inspiring and emotional story.

Animated maps were created to show the route of his marathon alongside important dates. Videos, images, soundtracks, and audio narratives were also edited and embedded to tell this story.

Application

  • Adobe Animate, Illustrator, Photoshop, and Premiere
  • Esri ArcGIS Online

Data

  • The Terry Fox Foundation
  • Basemap, Esri
