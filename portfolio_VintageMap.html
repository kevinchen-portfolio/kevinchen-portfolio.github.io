About

This project involved the recreation of a historical topographic map and employed current GIS data and modern cartography technologies. The topographic data were aligned and adjusted with MAPublisher.

Map size, scale, projection, and fonts were selected based upon their similarity to the original work. Various effects and textures in Illustrator were applied to imitate the historical style of the original map.

Application

  • Adobe Illustrator
  • Avenza MAPublisher
  • Esri ArcGIS Pro

Data
