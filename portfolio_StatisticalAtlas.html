About

This project involved the production of a statistical atlas of Nova Scotia. Statistical mapping techniques, such as creating population pyramids, dot density, proportional and graduated symbols have been used to provide comprehensive information to map readers.

Plate 1

Population by Age and Gender in the province of Nova Scotia and across the country.

Population distribution in Nova Scotia.

Plate 3

Proportional and Graduated Symbol maps showing the population by county.

Plate 4

Choropleth Maps showing the change of the population by county.

Application

  • Esri ArcMap, Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop, Microsoft Excel

Data

  • Boundary Files, Dissemination Area Boundary File, 2016 Census, Statistics Canada Catalogue.
  • Census Profile.2016 Census. Statistics Canada
