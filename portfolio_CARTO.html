About

This web map (click here to view) shows motor vehicle collisions in New York City from January 2019 to December 2020. The dataset used to create the map contains about three hundred thousand accidents from police-reported vehicle collisions in which someone was either injured, killed or damages of at least 1,000 dollars were incurred.

Animation and a heat map were embedded to show the data from different perspectives. The dynamic widgets allow users to identify the frequency of traffic accidents in a particular borough at a particular time.
