  1. Home

About

This map of Victoria was generalized using data from two different scales to a final scale of 1:100,000. Geodatabase XML and style files were managed to maintain data consistency, improve productivity, and ultimately produce an industry-standard topographic map.

Hillshade images were created to enhance the visualization of natural terrain.

Application

  • Esri ArcGIS Pro
  • Adobe Photoshop

Data

  • Natural Resources Canada
    (1: 50,000 scale)
  • U.S. Geological Survey
    (1: 24,000 scale)
  • DataBC

Details

Created modules in Esri's ModelBuilder to automate data manipulation.

Used graphic conflicts, editing toolsets, and Feature Outline Masks to increase the visibility of the map on a small scale.

Details on symbology design , such as reefs and embankments, to provide map readers with more information.

Designed and managed symbols and style files to maintain data consistency and improve productivity.
