About Kevin Chen

Kevin is a GIS engineer, responsible for maintaining, analyzing, and visualizing data to provide high-speed Internet solutions for rural Nova Scotia.

He graduated as valedictorian from the Geographic Sciences Program at the Centre of Geographic Sciences (Cartography concentration), Nova Scotia Community College, in 2021.

He has worked as a fibre network designer in the telecom industry in Beijing, China.

After relocating to Canada, he became passionate about cartography, graphic design, and data visualization.
