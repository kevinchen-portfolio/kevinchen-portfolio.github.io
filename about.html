About Kevin Chen

Kevin is a student in the two-year Geographic Sciences Program (Cartography concentration) at Centre of Geographic Sciences in Lawrencetown, Nova Scotia.

He has worked as a fiber network designer in the telecom industry in Beijing, China for eight years. After relocating to Canada, he became passionate about data analysis, visualisation and map design.

His goal is to start a meaningful career in Cartography and GIS!
