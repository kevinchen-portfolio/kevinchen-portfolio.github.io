About

This interactive map (click here to view) uses Tableau to show the global statistics of health expenditures and health worker numbers during the past 20 years.

The basemap was customized in Mapbox Studio. The data were manipulated in Tableau using functions such as join, clean, and pivot data in various formats to facilitate data analysis. It combines maps, dashboards, and tooltips with multiple data source connections to visualize data and graphical information.
